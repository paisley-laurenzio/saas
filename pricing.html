FAQs

Are recipes for individual portions or are they large enough for a full family?
Each recipe specifies the number of portions it creates so that you are able to adjust accordingly.

Are the ingredients required for recipes affordable?
We make your meal plans as affordable as possible for you. You have the option of specifying a budget when you create a profile, which will determine the recipes your meal plan provides you.

How does the monthly payment work?
Once you have created your account you will receive notifications five days before the beginning of each month asking you if you want to refresh your subscription and linking you to a payment page. You will be given a grace of five days after the beginning of a new month before you will loose access to your meal plan if you do not make your payment.
